The MyndAtlas project is an experiment, currently mostly chaos.

This is an experiment in documenting some thoughts and ideas I'm developing regarding the creation and use of note-systems in a way that I hope will ultimately be accessible to others, though it's definitely not there yet. The aim is somewhere between "leaving a trail of breadcrumbs" and "working with the garage door up". (Related: Digital Gardens and Learning in Public.)

Have a look at Welcome to the MyndAtlas Project if you haven't seen it already, check the Status of the MyndAtlas Project which may or may not have been updated recently, or see the notes by most recently changed via the Notes Feed .
