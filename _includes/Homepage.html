The project: to document my forays into the development of a practice for tool-supported thinking. The tool is a note-system. (In the vein of a Second Brain, a zettelkasten, or evergreen notes.)

It is an attempt to scaffold my ungovernable brain through big projects by working in a way that helps sporadic and intermittent work accumulate usefully.

I hope it will ultimately be accessible to others, but it isn't yet. The aim is somewhere between "leaving a trail of breadcrumbs" and "working with the garage door up". (Related: Digital Gardens and Learning in Public.)
