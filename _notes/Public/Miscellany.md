# Miscellany

This is where other notes that don't fit with the larger knowledge work aspect of the note-system, or with the planner, branch off. This is where I would put/find a recipe collection or the measurements of a window needed for ordering curtains or contact information for an insurance agent and that sort of thing.

Those familiar with "zettelkasten" or "Evergreen Notes" concepts might recognise this, along with the [[Planner]], and possibly the [[Bibliography]] as being "outside" those systems. I still find it handy to have them accessible within my MyndAtlas vault, so this is where I put them.
